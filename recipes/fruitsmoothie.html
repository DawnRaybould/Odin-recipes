Fruit Smoothie

How to make a fruit smoothie

Fruit smoothies are not only delecious, but they are nutritious as well. They're a great summertime treat, and super easy to make.

This recipe is for a strawberry smoothie, but you can substitute other fruits, like raspberries. Expirement to try different fruits and see which one you like best. Here's how to make one:

You will need:

  • A blender.
  • A tall glass.
  • 1 cup vanilla yogurt.
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries.
  • 1 frozen banana.
  • 1/4 cup orange juice.

To make the smoothie:

  1. Using your blender's puree setting, blend the yogurt, strawberries, banana and orange juice until smooth.
  2. Pour into glass and enjoy!
