How to make a grilled cheese sandwich

A grilled cheese sandwich is a quick and easy meal for lunchtime or a snack. It is delicious on its own or paired with tomato soup. Here's how to make one:

You will need:

  • A non-stick frying pan.
  • A spatula.
  • Two slices of bread. White bread works best, but any favorite sliced bread will work.
  • Softened butter or margarine.
  • 2 Velveeta cheese slices, or any brand sliced American cheese.

To make the sandwich:

  1. Place frying pan on medium heat.
  2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice into fryng pan butter side down.
  4. Place slices of cheese onto bread.
  5. Place second slice of bread on top of cheese, butter side up.
  6. Let cook for about a minute, then gently lift up edge of sandwich with the spatula to check if it's done on that side. Keep checking until that side is goldben brown.
  7. Then using the spatula, flip sandwich and repeat last step until both sides are golden brown.
  8. Use spatula to transfir to a plate and enjoy!
