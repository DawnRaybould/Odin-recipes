Scrambled Eggs

How to make scrambled eggs

Scrambled eggs are easy to make, so they are a perfect way for new cooks to gain confidence in the kitchen. Plus, they taste good!

Here is a simple recipe that makes a perfect dish of creamy scrambled eggs. Impress your friends and family with your new-found culinary abilities.

You will need:

  • A non-stick frying pan
  • A small bowl
  • A spatula
  • 2 large eggs
  • Butter or margarine
  • 2 table-spoons milk or half and half
  • Salt and pepper to taste

To make the eggs:

  1. Put the frying pan on low-medium heat and melt a dollup of butter.
  2. In a small bowl, crack open the eggs, taking care that none of the shell ends up in the bowl.
  3. Add approximately 2 table-spoons of milk or half and half.
  4. Break apart the yolks, then beat with a whisk just until the mixture is even and no large chunks of yolk are visible.
  5. Slowly pour the egg mixture into the pan. Then add the salt and pepper.
  6. Take the edge of the spatula and slowly scrape it through the mixture from one side of the pan to the other. Repeat, gently scraping the eggs until they start forming solid folds. Continute folding gently until eggs are no longer liquid, but still shiney.
  7. Scoop the eggs onto a plate and enjoy!
